Apple Walnut Salad with Creamy Lemon Dressing

Improv Oven Classic Recipe

A delicious and easy recipe that comes together quickly with simple ingredients.

Prep
15 mins
Cook
20 mins
Total
35 mins
Serves
4

Ingredients

  • 4 cups arugula
  • 2 stalks celery, sliced thin
  • 1 Gala apple, sliced thin
  • 1/2 cup whole walnuts
  • For Dressing
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 teaspoons white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. 1In a small mixing bowl crush together garlic, salt, pepper and lemon juice to form a paste.
  2. 2Add sour cream, continue mixing.
  3. 3Add white wine vinegar and mix until well combined.
💡 Improv Tip

Feel free to improvise — swap ingredients based on what you have. That's what Improv Oven is all about.
