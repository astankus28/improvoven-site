Simple Healthy Salad with Protein (Black Bean & Chicken Bowl)

Published May 4, 2026 · Improv Oven

This simple healthy salad with protein is the kind of meal that makes you feel like you actually have your life together — and it comes together in under 30 minutes with stuff you probably already have. We're talking tender seasoned chicken, hearty black beans, crisp romaine, and a zippy lime dressing that tastes like it came from a little café in Little Havana. It's filling, fresh, and genuinely good for you without making you feel like you're being punished.

Prep
10 mins
Cook
20 mins
Total
30 mins
Serves
4

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless skinless chicken breasts (about 1 lb)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 large head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup frozen corn, thawed (or canned, drained)
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil (for dressing)
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice (about 1 large lime)
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin (for dressing)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional but highly recommended)

Instructions

  1. 1Pat your chicken breasts dry with a paper towel — this is the move that gets you a nice golden crust instead of sad steamed chicken. Mix together the cumin, garlic powder, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper in a small bowl, then rub that seasoning all over both sides of the chicken.
  2. 2Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Once it's hot and shimmering, lay the chicken in and don't touch it for 5-6 minutes. Flip once and cook another 5-6 minutes until cooked through (internal temp of 165°F). Pull it off the heat and let it rest for 5 minutes — resting is not optional, it keeps your chicken juicy.
  3. 3While the chicken rests, make your lime dressing. Whisk together 3 tablespoons olive oil, lime juice, honey, cumin, and a pinch of salt and pepper in a small bowl or jar. Taste it — it should be bright, a little tangy, barely sweet. Adjust as needed.
  4. 4Warm your black beans in a small saucepan over low heat with a pinch of cumin and salt, or just microwave them for 90 seconds. Either way works, we're not gatekeeping here.
  5. 5Slice your rested chicken against the grain into strips. Build your salad bowls: start with a generous base of romaine, then layer on the cherry tomatoes, red onion, corn, warm black beans, and avocado slices.
  6. 6Fan the chicken slices over the top, drizzle everything with that lime dressing, and hit it with fresh cilantro if you've got it. Serve immediately and accept the compliments graciously.
💡 Improv Tip

No chicken? No problem — this salad works beautifully with a can of chickpeas, leftover rotisserie chicken, or even a couple of fried eggs on top. The black beans alone already give you solid protein, so work with what your fridge is offering you today.
