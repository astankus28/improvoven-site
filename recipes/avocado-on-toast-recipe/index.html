Avocado On Toast Recipe

Improv Oven Classic Recipe

Forget the café prices—this avocado toast crushes it at home for a fraction of the cost. We're piling fresh avocado, basil pesto, and spinach onto crispy whole grain bread, topped with cherry tomatoes. Twenty minutes from kitchen to table, and you've got a seriously satisfying meal that feeds four.

Prep
15 mins
Cook
20 mins
Total
35 mins
Serves
4

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of whole grain bread
  • 2 avocados
  • 2 tbs basil pesto
  • 2 tbs chopped spinach
  • 4 cherry tomatoes, sliced in half
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. 1Toast bread to desired crispness
  2. 2Cut avocados in half, removing seeds and scooping out each half in a bowl
  3. 3Add pesto and spinach and mix together
  4. 4Spread avocado mixture on toast and top with cherry tomatoes
  5. 5Add salt and pepper as needed
💡 Improv Tip

Don't skip toasting the bread—it adds crucial crunch that keeps your toast from getting soggy when the avocado spreads on. Use firm (not overripe) avocados for the best texture.
