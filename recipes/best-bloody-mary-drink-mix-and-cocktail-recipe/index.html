Best Bloody Mary Drink Mix And Cocktail Recipe

Improv Oven Classic Recipe

Forget the canned mixes—we're making a clarified Bloody Mary base from scratch that costs pennies and tastes infinitely better. This method gives you a clean, sophisticated mix that works for brunch cocktails or as a standalone sipper. Ready in just 35 minutes.

Prep
15 mins
Cook
20 mins
Total
35 mins
Serves
4

Ingredients

  • Bloody Mary drink mix
  • 2 ripe tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons celery salt
  • 2 teaspoons herbs de Provence
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1 shallot
  • 1 radish
  • 1 tablespoon horseradish
  • 0.75 oz Worcestershire sauce
  • 1.5 oz lemon juice
  • 0.75 oz habanero hot sauce
  • Spirits
  • 1.5–2 oz spirit of choice (vodka for a Bloody Mary, tequila for a Bloody Maria, or gin for a Red Snapper)
  • Optional garnishes
  • Carrots
  • Capers
  • Cucumber
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Celery stalk
  • Olives
  • Lemon wheels

Instructions

  1. 1Take Lemon Juice, Worcestershire, and tomatoes and blend in a blender for 1 minute or until completely liquified.
  2. 2Pour liquid mixture into a pot, add rest of ingredients, and set to boil.
  3. 3Once brought to a boil turn off heat and immediately strain through a cheese cloth.
  4. 4The result should be a thin, clarified, and lightly bodied Bloody Mary base. Allow to cool for 15-20 minutes.
  5. 5Add 1.5 oz. to 2 oz. spirit of your choice (Vodka for a Bloody Mary, Tequila for a Bloody Maria, or Gin for a Red Snapper), and serve over ice.
  6. 6Garnish by rimming your glass first with run of your choice (sea salt, celery salt, spicy salt, etc ...), then inserting carrots, capers, cucumber, cherry tomatoes, celery stalk, olives, and lemon wheels. Enjoy!
💡 Improv Tip

Cheesecloth is key for that silky-smooth texture, but a fine mesh strainer lined with paper towels works just as well if you don't have it on hand.
