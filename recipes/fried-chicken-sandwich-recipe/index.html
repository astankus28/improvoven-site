Fried Chicken Sandwich Recipe

Improv Oven Classic Recipe

This fried chicken sandwich proves you don't need a mile-long ingredient list to make restaurant-quality comfort food. With just chicken, Italian herb bread crumbs, and oil, you're 35 minutes away from a golden, crispy sandwich that'll make you forget takeout even exists.

Prep
15 mins
Cook
20 mins
Total
35 mins
Serves
4

Instructions

  1. 1Take out your three ingredients: Vegetable Oil, Italian Herbs Infused Bread Crumbs & Chicken. (Optional addition, your favorite side sauce for accompaniment.)
  2. 2Place 1 part oil in a mixing bowl, and bread crumbs in another mixing bowl. Make sure chicken is fully thawed and patted down dry.
  3. 3Heat up a pan on the stovetop, on medium, with the other part of vegetable oil. Wait until it gets lightly bubbly and steaming, heat to 350 degrees.
  4. 4Take a thawed, chicken thigh and dredge it in the oil in the bowl you've set aside. Remove the chicken from the oil and then dip it in the breadcrumbs and be sure to completely coat the chicken thigh with the breadcrumbs. Repeat the process until each piece is full coated with breadcrumbs.
  5. 5Cook in frying pan equally on each side until fully cooked or internal temperature reaches 165 degrees. About 10 minutes.
  6. 6Serve hot on buns, top with coleslaw, pickles, and your favorite sauce.
💡 Improv Tip

Pat your chicken completely dry before breading—moisture is the enemy of crispiness. Let your oil hit 350°F and listen for that gentle sizzle; it's your sign you're in the danger zone for perfect golden crust.
