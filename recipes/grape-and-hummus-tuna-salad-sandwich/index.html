Grape and Hummus Tuna Salad Sandwich

Improv Oven Classic Recipe

Forget boring tuna salad—hummus swaps in for mayo and brings creaminess without the heaviness. Fresh grapes add sweetness and snap that makes this sandwich feel special, not sad desk lunch. It's ready in 20 minutes and costs next to nothing.
