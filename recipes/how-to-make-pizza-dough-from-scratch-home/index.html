How to Make Homemade Pizza Dough from Scratch

Improv Oven Classic Recipe

Skip the frozen dough aisle—homemade pizza base costs pennies and tastes infinitely better. This no-fuss method uses a standing mixer to handle the work, giving you restaurant-quality dough in under 40 minutes. Perfect for topping with whatever's in your fridge.

Prep
15 mins
Cook
20 mins
Total
35 mins
Serves
4

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbs sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbs shortening
  • 1 1/4 cups warm water
  • 1 tbs active dry yeast
  • 2tbs vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. 1Attach dough hook to standing mixer
  2. 2In standing mixer bowl add sugar, salt, yeast, shortening and water in that order. Turn mixer on.
  3. 3Add flour. Let dough hook stir mixture until flour starts to come together.
  4. 4Drizzle oil on sides of bowl and continue mixing until dough begins to form. Scrape remaining flour off sides of bowl and mix in with hands. Dough should not be sticky.
  5. 5Cover bowl with kitchen towel and set in warm dry place to let rise, about 45 minutes
  6. 6Remove risen dough from bowl and form ball with hands. Ready to use..
💡 Improv Tip

Don't skip the vegetable oil drizzle on the bowl sides—it prevents sticking and helps the dough release cleanly. Use warm (not hot) water; too hot kills the yeast and ruins your rise.
