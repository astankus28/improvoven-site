Simple Chicken Salad Sandwich Meal Prep for Easy Weeknight Dinners

Published April 22, 2026 · Improv Oven

This simple chicken salad sandwich meal prep is the move when you want something satisfying on the table fast without starting from zero every night. We're talking creamy, herby chicken salad loaded with crunch, made in one big batch so dinner is basically done before you even sit down. Very Miami of us — work smart, eat good, no stress.

Prep
10 mins
Cook
20 mins
Total
30 mins
Serves
4

Ingredients

  • 2 large chicken breasts (about 1.5 lbs)
  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon yellow mustard
  • 2 stalks celery, finely chopped
  • 1/4 red onion, finely diced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 8 slices sturdy sandwich bread or 4 bolillo rolls
  • 4 large lettuce leaves (romaine or butter lettuce)
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 1 avocado, sliced (optional but highly recommended)

Instructions

  1. 1Place the chicken breasts in a medium pot and cover with cold water by about an inch. Add a big pinch of salt, a few peppercorns if you have them, and bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Once boiling, reduce to a gentle simmer and cook for 15 to 18 minutes until the chicken is cooked through with no pink in the center. Pull them out and let them rest on a cutting board for 5 minutes — don't skip this, it keeps the chicken juicy.
  2. 2While the chicken cools, mix together the mayonnaise, yellow mustard, lime juice, garlic powder, cumin, smoked paprika, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Give it a taste — this is your base dressing and it should already make you happy.
  3. 3Use two forks to shred the chicken right on the cutting board, or chop it into small bite-sized pieces if you prefer a chunkier texture. Either way works great here.
  4. 4Add the shredded chicken, chopped celery, red onion, and cilantro into the bowl with the dressing. Fold everything together until well combined and every piece of chicken is coated. Taste again and adjust salt, pepper, or lime juice as needed.
  5. 5If you're meal prepping, transfer the chicken salad to an airtight container and refrigerate. It keeps beautifully for up to 4 days, and honestly the flavor gets even better overnight as everything comes together.
  6. 6To build your dinner sandwiches, lay out your bread or toasted bolillo rolls. Add a generous scoop of chicken salad, then layer on the lettuce, tomato slices, and avocado. Serve right away with chips, a simple green salad, or sliced fruit on the side for a complete meal.
💡 Improv Tip

Listen — the cumin and smoked paprika are the secret weapons here. Most chicken salad recipes skip the spices and that's exactly why they taste like nothing. This little Latin-inspired twist is what makes people ask you for the recipe. Also, poaching the chicken yourself instead of using canned gives you way more flavor and it costs less per pound, which is very much the Improv Oven way.
