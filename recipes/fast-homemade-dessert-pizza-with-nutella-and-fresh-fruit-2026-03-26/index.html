Fast Homemade Dessert Pizza with Nutella and Fresh Fruit

Published March 26, 2026 · Improv Oven

This fast homemade pizza recipe gets a serious glow-up when you swap the sauce and cheese for Nutella, sliced strawberries, and a drizzle of honey — trust me, it works. I make this when I need something sweet on the table in under 30 minutes and the fruit in my fridge is starting to look at me sideways. It's the kind of dessert that makes people think you did something fancy when really you just cured your refrigerator blindness.

Prep
10 mins
Cook
12 mins
Total
22 mins
Serves
4

Ingredients

  • 1 can (13.8 oz) refrigerated pizza dough or store-bought flatbread
  • 1/3 cup Nutella or any chocolate hazelnut spread
  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, thinly sliced
  • 1 medium banana, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1/4 cup mini marshmallows (optional but very much recommended)
  • 1 tablespoon powdered sugar for dusting
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil or cooking spray for the pan

Instructions

  1. 1Preheat your oven to 425°F. Lightly grease a baking sheet or pizza pan with olive oil or cooking spray.
  2. 2Roll out your pizza dough onto the prepared pan into a rough 12-inch circle — don't stress about making it perfect, a rustic shape is part of the charm here.
  3. 3Pre-bake the bare dough for 5 minutes. This step is key — it keeps the crust from getting soggy once you add your toppings.
  4. 4Pull the crust out of the oven and let it cool for just 2 minutes so the Nutella doesn't melt into a puddle immediately.
  5. 5Spread the Nutella evenly across the crust, leaving about a half-inch border around the edges like you would with tomato sauce.
  6. 6Scatter the sliced strawberries and banana evenly over the Nutella. If you're using mini marshmallows, sprinkle them on now.
  7. 7Return the pizza to the oven and bake for another 6 to 7 minutes, until the edges of the crust are golden and any marshmallows are lightly toasted.
  8. 8Remove from the oven, drizzle honey all over the top, and finish with a light dusting of powdered sugar.
  9. 9Slice it up and serve warm. This one disappears fast — you have been warned.
💡 Improv Tip

Whatever fruit is looking a little too ripe in your fridge right now is the perfect fruit for this pizza — mango, peaches, raspberries, even canned mandarin oranges work beautifully. This recipe exists specifically to save that fruit from the trash, so use what you've got and don't overthink it.
