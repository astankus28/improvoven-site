Easy Snickerdoodles Cookie Recipe

Improv Oven Classic Recipe

Forget store-bought cookies. These homemade snickerdoodles are soft, buttery, and come together in under 35 minutes with everyday pantry staples. They're perfect for breakfast, dessert, or whenever you need a quick sweet fix without breaking the bank.

Prep
15 mins
Cook
20 mins
Total
35 mins
Serves
4

Ingredients

  • For Dough
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • For Cookie Dough Balls
  • 3 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons ground cinnamon

Instructions

  1. 1Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Beat butter at medium speed with standing electric mixer until creamy. Gradually add 2 cups sugar, beating well. Add eggs, milk, and vanilla extract, beating until mixture is fully combined.
  2. 2Combine flour, baking powder, and 2 tsp. cinnamon. Gradually add to butter mixture, beating at low speed until well combined.
  3. 3Add 3 Tbsp. sugar and 1 1/2 Tbsp. cinnamon in a separate small bowl. Roll dough mixture into 1 -inch balls, and roll into mixture. Place dough balls on parchment paper on baking sheets, and flatten slightly with your hands.
  4. 4Bake for 11 to 13 minutes or until lightly browned. Bake for 15 minutes for a crispier cookie. Cool on wire rack for about 30 minutes.
💡 Improv Tip

Don't skip rolling the dough balls in the cinnamon-sugar mixture—it creates that signature crackly, caramelized edge. Chill the dough for 10 minutes if it's too soft to handle.
