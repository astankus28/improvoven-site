Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Improv Oven ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website improvoven.com. This page explains our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

Improv Oven is a recipe blog. We do not require you to create an account to use this website. The only personal information we collect is your email address if you voluntarily subscribe to our recipe newsletter. We do not collect names or any other personally identifiable information unless you contact us directly.

Log Data

Like most websites, our hosting provider may automatically collect standard log data when you visit our site, including your browser type, pages visited, time and date of your visit, and your IP address. This data is used solely for analyzing site performance and is not linked to any personally identifiable information.

Email Communications

If you subscribe to our email newsletter, we will send you a daily recipe digest and weekly round-up emails. You can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email. We use MailerLite to manage our email list — your email address is stored securely on their servers and is never sold or shared with third parties.

Cookies

Improv Oven uses minimal cookies necessary for the website to function. We do not use tracking cookies or advertising cookies. Third-party services we use (such as Google Fonts) may set their own cookies subject to their own privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data subject to their own privacy policies:

  • Google Fonts — for typography
  • Cloudflare — for website hosting and performance
  • Pinterest — for recipe sharing and automated content publishing via the Pinterest API
  • MailerLite — for email newsletter management. View their privacy policy
  • Meta (Facebook & Instagram) — for automated recipe publishing to our Facebook Page and Instagram account via the Meta Graph API. View their privacy policy

Links to Other Sites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content or privacy practices of any third-party sites.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13 years of age and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date above.
