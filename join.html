Get Involved

You Belong Here.

Whether you have deep Lithuanian roots or are just discovering your heritage — our community is open to everyone. Join us, volunteer, or simply stay connected.

Ways to Get Involved
🤝
Become a Member
Formally join the Cleveland Lithuanian American Community. Members receive event updates, voting rights at annual meetings, and the satisfaction of supporting Lithuanian heritage in Northeast Ohio.
🙌
Volunteer
Help make our events happen. From setting up at the Lithuanian Cultural Garden to staffing the booth at One World Day — every pair of hands makes a difference. No experience needed, just enthusiasm.
📬
Stay Connected
Sign up for event announcements and community news. We send updates about upcoming events, cultural programs, and community happenings — no spam, just Lithuanian community news.
Express Your Interest
Fill out the form below and we'll be in touch. There's no obligation — just let us know how you'd like to be part of the community.
✓
Thank you!
We've received your interest and will be in touch soon. In the meantime, follow us on Facebook and Instagram to stay connected with the community.
